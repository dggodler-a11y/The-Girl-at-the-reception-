# The-Girl-at-the-reception-
A man checks into a strange, abandoned hotel for the night. Late knocks, shadows, and a mysterious receptionist blur reality and fear. Each choice uncovers fragments of a tragic past, leading to a chilling truth that follows him even after he leaves.
# Interactive Story Game

A browser-based interactive story told through scenes, images, and dialogue.
Players progress by clicking through episodes and making choices that affect tone and discovery.

## How to Play
- Open the website link
- Click to move through scenes
- Use on-screen buttons to continue the story
